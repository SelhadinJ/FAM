About Us

Welcome to Fam Med Trading Pvt.Ltd.Co. your premier pharmaceutical import company based in Addis Ababa. Our mission is to provide safe, effective, and affordable pharmaceutical products, laboratory supplies, medical devices, consumables, and other medical equipment to our customers throughout Ethiopia and the Horn. We are committed to delivering the highest level of customer service and satisfaction, and we strive to meet the unique needs of each of our clients.

As a leader in the healthcare industry, we pride ourselves on providing a diverse range of quality products at competitive prices. Our dedicated team of experts has extensive experience in sourcing and importing the latest products and technologies to meet the evolving needs of the Ethiopian healthcare sector. At Fam Med Trading Pvt.Ltd.Co. we understand the importance of innovation and are always looking for ways to improve the quality and accessibility of our products.
