Products

At Fam Med Trading Pvt.Ltd.Co., we offer a comprehensive range of high-quality pharmaceutical products, laboratory supplies, medical machines, consumables, and other medical equipment. Our product line includes a variety of hemodialysis equipment and supplies, such as hemodialysis machines, dialysis catheters, and dialysis fluids. We also offer a range of medical machines, including X-ray machines, ultrasound machines, patient monitors, ECG machines, defibrillators, and more.

To complement our medical machines, we provide a range of consumables such as syringes, needles, IV tubes, and catheters. In addition, our laboratory supplies and diagnostic tests are of the highest quality and sourced from reputable manufacturers worldwide.

For laboratory supplies, we provide a wide range of products including high-quality reagents, chemicals, and lab equipment from reputable manufacturers worldwide. Our laboratory supplies are used in various fields including microbiology, biochemistry, hematology, immunology, and more. We also offer a range of diagnostic tests and kits for various diseases including HIV, hepatitis, malaria, and COVID-19. These tests are of the highest quality and are sourced from reputable manufacturers worldwide.

For hemodialysis equipment and supplies, we offer a range of products including hemodialysis machines, dialyzers, dialysis catheters, and dialysis fluids. Our dialyzers are designed to effectively remove waste and excess fluid from the blood of patients with kidney failure. They are available in a range of sizes and materials to suit the needs of each individual patient.
